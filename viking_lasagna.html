Viking Lasagna

Description

Viking lasagna is a specialty of Southern Norway and it includes the typical ingredients of regular lasagna except also with lutefisk gnar gnar gnar.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • All the stuff inside of lasagna
  • Tomato sauce
  • That flat pasta
  • Meat
  • Ricotta cheese
  • LUTEFISK! GNAR GNAR GNAR GNAR GNAR >:D

Steps

  1. Put all of the lasagna in the pan
  2. Bake the lasagna in the oven
  3. COVER IT IN LUTEFISK GNAR GNAR GNAR GNAR
